Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Max Crafter YT website ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or updates
  • Communicate with us via email or social media
  • Participate in our community features

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Time and date of visit
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information
  • To send you updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To protect against fraudulent activities and ensure security

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent for specific purposes
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services, including social media platforms and video hosting services. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to processing of your personal information

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
